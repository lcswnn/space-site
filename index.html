Advocacy

    Support space education in local schools.

    Partner with educators, museums, and STEM organizations to integrate engaging space science programs into the curriculum. Provide funding for telescopes, planetarium visits, and hands-on workshops so students can explore astronomy, rocketry, and planetary science. Inspire curiosity about the universe and cultivate the next generation of scientists, engineers, and explorers.

    Promote planetary defense research.

    Advocate for funding and collaboration between government agencies, universities, and private companies to detect, track, and study near-Earth objects (NEOs). Support missions that test asteroid deflection techniques and improve early-warning systems, ensuring that humanity is prepared to prevent or mitigate potential asteroid impacts.

    Encourage open access to mission data.

    Push for transparency and free availability of scientific data from government and privately funded space missions. Open data fosters global collaboration, accelerates discovery, and allows educators, researchers, and citizen scientists to contribute to the exploration and understanding of space.
